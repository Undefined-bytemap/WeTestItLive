Privacy Policy

Overview

we test it LIVE provides free hardware testing tools without requiring any personal information. We prioritize your privacy and believe in being transparent about our data practices.

Data Collection

We collect minimal, anonymous analytics data to help improve the site:

  • Session timestamp
  • Browser user agent
  • Pages visited
  • Referring site
  • Browser language

We do not collect:

  • Personal information
  • IP addresses
  • Test results or inputs
  • Cookies or local storage data

How We Use Data

Analytics data is used for site improvement purposes and is not tied to individual users. This data helps us understand general usage patterns and improve the site's functionality, including:

  • Understanding which hardware tests are most used
  • Optimizing for different browsers and devices
  • Improving site navigation and usability
  • Planning future features and updates

Third-Party Services

Our site does not use any third-party analytics, advertising services, or tracking scripts. All tests run directly in your browser without sending information to external servers.

Data Security

All tests run entirely in your browser. Hardware test data (like camera feeds, microphone input, or keyboard strokes) is processed locally and is never transmitted to our servers or stored anywhere.

Data Retention

Basic analytics data is retained for a limited time for the sole purpose of improving the site's functionality. Since the data is anonymized and not linked to individuals, it poses no privacy risks.

Your Rights

Since we don't collect personal information, there's no personal data to access, modify, or delete. You can verify this by using your browser's developer tools to inspect network traffic during your visit.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy as needed. Significant changes will be announced on our homepage.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, please visit our FAQ page or find contact information on our About page.
