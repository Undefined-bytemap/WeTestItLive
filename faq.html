Frequently Asked Questions

What information does this site collect?

To help improve the site, we collect basic device information such as:

  • Device type and browser information
  • Pages visited

This information helps us understand which devices are most commonly using our tool, allowing us to prioritize features and improvements that benefit the majority of our users. For example, if we see many Chromebook users, we might focus on adding more Chrome OS-specific testing features.

We do not collect any personal information or store any data that could identify individual users or devices.

How do I grant camera permissions?

When you click "Test Cameras", your browser will prompt you to allow camera access. Click "Allow" to grant permission. If you accidentally deny permission, you can click the camera icon in your browser's address bar to change the setting.
